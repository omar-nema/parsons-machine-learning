Explore Palestinian Posters
The Palestine Poster Project is a collection over 10,000 posters related to Palestine, compiled by Dan Walsh. In this application, a set of 5000 posters created by Palestinian artists, and drawn from the PPP archive, are sorted by similarity using machine learning. Poster assets and metadata were taken from the Palestine Poster Project, curated by Dan Walsh, and span from 1930 to present day.

In the grid below, posters are sorted by similarity - posters close to each other share similar visual content, iconography, and artists (determined by a combination of machine learning methods - feature extraction and dimensional reduction).

All poster content is taken from the Palestine Poster Project. Sole credit for archiving content and labeling metadata goes to Dan Walsh and the PPP team.
